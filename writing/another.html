Another Insecurity

I’m shy around people I don’t know;
It doesn’t come off as quietness anymore
Rather I feel that my personality doesn’t show;
A “normal” front
Masks my old insecurities.

My discomfort is confusing
It isn’t enough that I’m having fun?
An old voice calls out profusely
Being interesting is what you need
To be in security.

Social anxiety was a beast previously slain
What’s left is a need to prove something.
Though a previous one is resolved, I remain
Continuously in a process of
Collecting another insecurity.
