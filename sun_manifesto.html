INTRODUCTION

FIRST AND FOREMOST: DISREGARD THE DISCLAIMER.

This is not a work of fiction. This is a genuine product of what the members of OCCULT believe.

The allegory of the cave was first introduced to mankind by the Greek philosopher Plato. The concept is as follows: for most of history, humans were prisoners chained to the ceiling, facing a cavern wall lit by a large fire. The wall projected distorted shadows of figures from the outside world, a faint whisper of reality. The OCCULT believes that somewhere in our timeline however, the chains were cut. With the introduction of science, philosophy, and mathematics, mankind should have exited the cave and enjoyed the beauty of reality freely. Unfortunately, most of mankind is held back by a debilitating irrational fear of the sun, unwilling to face reality and let their eyes adjust to the outside world.

The OCCULT aims to equip the ordinary man with tools to drag himself out of the cave.

Too many people are living in ignorance, afraid to turn their eyes to reality and instead seeking false comfort from illusions. The pitiful consequence of this is that to lead this life is to eventually wither away in the cave (ironically, from lack of sunlight). We even more ironically complain later that we simply weren’t fortunate enough to have obtained the tools necessary to ascend from the cave.

Humanity should no longer be stuck in our primitive, outdated ways of living. We must modernize and adapt to thrive in the world as it is in the twenty-first century.

WHY SHOULD WE WASTE OUR LIVES LIVING SOMETHING THAT ISN’T REAL?

The following is everything that is wrong with the world, and how OCCULT would fix things. At the root of it all, people need to look inwards and question who they actually are. This is a very PAINFUL process, and only the strong minded will make it to the other side. We must place an emphasis here on PAINFUL, because the following truths provided by the sun are meant to scorch one to the core. Those who are too cowardly to even attempt to leave the cave are what is wrong with society.

Though OCCULT takes an aggressive stance in this document, we do so out of necessity and out of good will. One cannot experience pleasure without displeasure first.
