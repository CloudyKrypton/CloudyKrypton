PREFACE: THE PROBLEM OF NIHILISM

☀

OCCULT recognizes the problem of nihilism in regards to establishing proper metaphysical theories. We generally agree with the principle that most things can be reduced to nothing. However, it is easy and not very useful to prove that everything is nothing, and much more difficult but much more practical to attempt to generate something from nothing. Thus, as we go through the list of metaphysical topics in the rest of the manifesto (read: the other chapters of this series), we will acknowledge each topic’s “nothingness” here, and also describe the scope which we are looking at the problem through.

  1. Identity of objects - true numerical identity, i.e. object x is equal to object y can only exist instantaneously. OCCULT rejects true numerical identity as the concept of identity however, because clearly there exists a social identity that is not strictly numerical, and this is what we aim to determine. We believe that definitions exist on primarily social axioms, and will treat identity as such.

That person who believes in nothing can produce no action.
